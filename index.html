Srila Bhaktivinoda Thakur Janmasthan Development Project

This project, a humble yet monumental offering to our ācāryas, is being undertaken in Bir Nagar, the sacred birthplace of Śrīla Bhaktivinoda Ṭhākura. This project, envisioned by our Founder-Ācārya His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupāda, is dedicated to glorifying the life, teachings, and legacy of Śrīla Bhaktivinoda Ṭhākura, the pioneer of the modern bhakti movement.

This temple and cultural center will serve as a beacon of Kṛṣṇa consciousness, carrying forward the vision of Śrīla Bhaktivinoda Ṭhākura to enlighten the world with pure devotion. Generations to come will be able to visit Bir Nagar, witness his glories, and experience the transformative power of his mission.

The Sacred Timeline

A journey of devotion, rising from the earth to the sky. Witness the phases of construction.

2024: The Foundation

TARGET: Rs 5 CR • STATUS: COMPLETED

The earth was blessed, and the deep piling work was completed. The roots of the temple are now set firm in the holy dhama.

CURRENT PHASE

2025: Rising Structures

The visible form of the Lord's palace begins to rise. Pillars, beams, and the first floor slab are currently under construction.

2026: The Domes

TARGET: Rs 8 CR • STATUS: UPCOMING

The grand domes will be cast, crowning the temple. Intricate stone carving work begins on the exterior facade.

2027: Deity Installation Prep

TARGET: Rs 6 CR • STATUS: UPCOMING

Interior finishing, marble laying, and the preparation of the golden altar for the Lordships.
