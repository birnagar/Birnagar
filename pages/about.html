The Legacy of Birnagar

Birnagar, formerly known as Ula, holds immense significance in Gaudiya Vaishnavism. It is here that the great acharya, Srila Bhaktivinoda Thakur, appeared in 1838 to reclaim the lost essence of pure devotional service.

The Thakur's childhood home was a place of opulence and learning, yet his heart was always yearning for the absolute truth. Today, Birnagar Temple Project is dedicated to reconstructing this site to its former glory, serving as a beacon of spiritual education.

Our Mission

  • To reconstruct the birthplace temple of Srila Bhaktivinoda Thakur.
  • To propagate the chanting of the Holy Names of Krishna.
  • To distribute sanctified food (Prasadam) to the local community.
